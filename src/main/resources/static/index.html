Selected Tourist Attractions

  • Big Ben - It is the 3rd largest free-standing clock tower in the world. The clock has become a symbol of England and London.
  • The British Museum - A public museum dedicated to human history, art, and culture located in the Bloomsbury area of London.
  • Windsor Castle - Windsor Castle is often called the largest and oldest inhabited castle in the world.
  • Buckingham Palace - The official residence of the British monarch in London, Buckingham Palace is an iconic symbol of the British monarchy.
